Curriculum and Learning plan

The Curriculum and learning plans at Mahavana Residential school for childern with Intellectual disabilities are aligned with prescribed Curriculum of NIEPID ( National institute for the Empowerment of persons with Intellectual Disabilities). The teaching method are designed to cater to the needs of Intellectually challenged Children keeping in mind the standard pace at which they could comfortably learn and grow. The school is adequately equipped with several apparatus and instruments for hands-on learning. Training also imparted in yoga, Meditation, folk dance, Singing, Bhajans, etc, children also enjoy games, gardening and other fun activiteis

Vocation training

Vocational training is given to enable children to go home as functioning and supportive family member. The childeren are given various kinds of training to garner vocational skills that can help them and their families latern on in life. The include skills such as candle making, chalk maing soft toy making, paper cover and greeting card making. These skills are honed over period of time and help the child tremendously. The goal is for Children to be able to live indepently with diginity and have vocational skills to prove proudly in the community. A subsidy is provided by the Government and can be made into profilitable business but the am is to keep them occupied and engaged.
