About Our School

Mahavana Residential School for Children with Intellectual Disabilities is a village-based institution aimed at empowering children with disabilities between 6 and 16 years of age. This school was started in June 2023 in a rented building in Molakalmuru town and shifted its operations to its own new building in Shubodayanagar at Hanagal in Chitradurga District in June 2024. We believe that all children, including those with intellectual disabilities, deserve a fair chance at life. Our residential school shelters, feeds, and nurtures young children who need special attention and care. All our services are free, and we do not collect any fee or deposit for the services we render. Voluntary donations from anyone are welcome.

OUR TEACHERS, OUR PRIDE

For many, teaching may be a profession, but to our teachers, teaching is a passion and devotion. All our teachers are recognized by the Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI) and are well-trained to care for young children.

ADDITIONAL PROFESSIONALS

The additional staff include highly experienced and skilled professionals in their respective fields. These well-qualified professionals are: Dance Teachers, Psychologists, Physicians, Pediatricians, and Speech Therapists. The helping staff consists of experienced, trained, and compassionate individuals who work tirelessly on children's day-to-day needs.

Our Facility

Our state-of-the-art facility is designed to provide a safe and stimulating environment for our children. With specialized classrooms, therapy rooms, and recreational areas, we ensure that every child's needs are met.
